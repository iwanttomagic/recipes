Vegetarian Nacho Recipe

Ingredients:
1 bg Tortilla Chips
1 cup grated Cheddar cheese
8 oz black olives
1/2 medium onion sliced
3 cloves garlic minced
1 tomato diced
16 oz can of black beans
1 tbs oil
Sour Cream
Salsa
Guacamole

Instructions:
1.) Preheat oven to 350 F
2.) Sate onion in oil until golden brown, set aside
3.) Poor bag of chips on baking sheet
4.) Sprinkle half of cheese over chips
5.) Spread onion, garlic, beans, tomato, and olives over chips and cheese
6.) Spread rest of cheese over the top
7.) Place in oven for 5-10 minutes, or until cheese melts
8.) Remove and let sit while you put the Sour Cream, Salsa and Guacamole in bowls
9.) Eat on plates with dips
